Overview Project

Mental health is a critical aspect of overall well-being, yet many people hesitate to seek help due to various barriers such as stigma, cost, or lack of access to professional care. HearMePal aims to bridge this gap by offering a supportive, non-judgmental space where users can express their feelings and receive guidance.
