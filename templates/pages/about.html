About HearMePal

Welcome to HearMePal, your companion in mental health support. Our mission is to make mental health support accessible and approachable through advanced technology.

What is HearMePal?

Mental Health Assitant

HearMePal is a mental health chatbot created to offer support and guidance to individuals experiencing anxiety and depression. It engages in conversations similar to those with a therapist, providing a safe and supportive environment where users can express their feelings. Utilizing advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) algorithms.

Our Mission

Break The Gap

Mental health is a critical aspect of overall well-being, yet many people hesitate to seek help due to various barriers such as stigma, cost, or lack of access to professional care. HearMePal aims to bridge this gap by offering a supportive, non-judgmental space where users can express their feelings and receive guidance.
